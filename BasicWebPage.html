Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an indian international cricketer who plays Test and One Day International cricket for the indian national team. A former captain in all formats, Kohli retired from Twenty20 International following India's win at the 2024 T20 World Cup. He represents Royal Challengers Bengaluru in the Indian Premier League.
